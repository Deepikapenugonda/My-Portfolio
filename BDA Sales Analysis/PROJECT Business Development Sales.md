**ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS**



* **Why is Australia underperforming?**

*Possible reasons include lower brand awareness, stronger local competition, pricing misalignment with local market, or insufficient BD presence in the region*

* **Why is Online only marginally ahead?**

*Indicates strong in-store customer loyalty possibly due to product categories like Furniture where customers prefer physical inspection before purchase.*

* **Why do Medium priority orders generate more revenue?**

*Planned purchases tend to have higher cart values as customers add multiple items. Urgent high priority orders are typically single item emergency purchases with lower value.*
