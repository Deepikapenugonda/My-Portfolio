**Title:** IBM HR Employee Attrition Analysis

**Business Problem:**

IBM is experiencing a 16% employee attrition rate which is higher than the industry standard of 10%. This analysis identifies key reasons and recommends solutions.

**Key Findings:**

R\&D department accounts for 56% of all attrition

Employees aged 26-35 are leaving the most (49%)

Employees who left earned 30% less than those who stayed

Lowest job satisfaction employees are leaving most

**Recommendations:**

Increase salaries for R\&D department employees immediately

Create career growth programs for 26-35 age group

Conduct satisfaction surveys quarterly to identify unhappy employees early

Introduce retention bonuses for high performing R\&D staff
